INTERNATIONAL FREIGHT FORWARDING

Selecting the most efficient and cost effective transportation option can be challenging. At Delmar, we work with you to create solutions that are flexible and result in the most highly reliable and customizable service in the industry.

As an asset light global logistics provider, Delmar partners with key air, ocean and ground carriers to provide integrated global transportation solutions. These partnerships allow for greater leverage in both space allocation and pricing, along with the ability to offer more consistent transit and lift options.

In addition, we offer multimodal transportation solutions, such as Sea/Air and Air/Sea. These multimodal options provide flexibility in both timing and price. For urgent or specialized shipments, we have customizable logistics options to keep your cargo visible and moving through your supply chain.

To best support your ever-changing logistics needs, we are continuously evolving our transportation services with the development of value added solutions, such as: capacity programs, direct delivery, time definite services, cross dock, temperature control, garment on hanger, charters and project cargo services.

Air

As an IATA (International Air Transportation Association) agent for all major airlines, we provide a complete package of air logistics solutions. We understand that air freight is a premium and costly service, so our program focuses on quick transit and constant communication, combined with the ability to seize cost saving opportunities such as consolidation and intermodal options.

Ocean

As an FMC (Federal Maritime Commission) approved & licensed Ocean Transportation Intermediary and NVOCC, we offer a full array of ocean freight services including ocean freight forwarding and consolidation, and LCL or FCL container management. Using our contractual relationships with steamship lines, we offer flexible scheduling and competitive rates.

CUSTOMS BROKERAGE SERVICES

Canadian, American, Mexican & Chinese

In order for you to realize significant and quantifiable savings, Delmar provides innovative approaches to customs programs. Our approach is to determine how value can best be added to your processes. We look at your customs "value stream," which includes compliance, release and analysis, as a way of effectively managing cost.

As a compliance-driven organization, we are positioned to understand and address the intricacies of trade regulations and agreements at the global, regional, national and local levels. We focus on three key elements to ensure adherence to all customs requirements and promote process-strict compliance:

  • Regulations.
  • Reliable release timing.
  • Value added data.

We strive to be welcomed and respected by all levels of government, in many cases Delmar is invited to participate in pilot programs with Customs and is often invited to give input on program analysis.

By utilizing Delmar for your customs needs, you can better manage your programs and meet the requirements of an ever-changing regulatory environment.

Delmar provides Licensed Customs Brokerage service throughout Canada, The United States and Mexico.


Customs Release & Compliance

Delmar has a team of Trade & Customs Specialists ready to assist importers in managing all factors related to commercial imports. Our experienced professionals will help ensure that you are prepared for a formal customs audit and can be on site to assist as well. Delmar can audit, prepare or submit drawbacks and refund claims on your behalf for customs duties assessed on import declarations.

  • File your import, export and security declarations efficiently electronically.
  • Enhance existing customs compliance programs, such as free trade zones, special regimes, border crossing and trade preferences.
  • Minimize risk and future potential costs of non-compliance.

OUR TECHNOLOGY

Cargo Management, Purchase Order & Tracking

Whether you are an importer, an exporter, or both, Delmar is here to help you move your order through your supply chain with uncompromising attention to detail. It is the management of those details that allow for continuous improvements in your supply chain.

The Delmar Cargo Management System or DCMS™ is our global proprietary cargo management platform. It couples technology with hands-on support from experienced professionals. You decide what best suits your organizations requirements and we’ll help you customize DCMS™ to your needs. The Delmar platform is unique, easy to use and simple to initiate.

Basic Functions

  • Vendors & Purchase Order management
  • Cargo booking, approval & validation
  • Rate & cost management and optimization
  • Automated messaging alerts
  • Exception based reporting, analytics and collaboration

Advanced Features

  • Integrated customs clearance & compliance management
  • Advance trade data & security program management
  • Work flow processes and image/document storage
  • Data interchange & integration
  • Dedicated secure online Delmar data cloud (SaaS)
  • Share & collaborate alerts, reports and data feeds
  • Network with other systems, partners, and associates

Delmar's online Tracking Tools give you the peace of mind that comes with complete transparency. DCMS™ technology provides you easy, instant access to your freight’s status, allowing you to follow a single shipment in real-time. You can choose between Standard Tracking and advanced Tracking.

Tracking

  • Track a single shipment online through Delmar’s secure, proprietary process.
  • Access basic shipment information and receive updates on significant steps and procedures (e.g., receipt of goods, international departure, international arrival, delivery completed).

Advanced Tracking

  • Conduct advanced searches for orders and shipments with date ranges, events and multiple references.
  • Generate reports with complete, transparent shipment data.
